About Me

I am Eryn Gabriel Tallador, a student from De La Salle University - Manila currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Interactive Entertainment - Major in Game Development. I am in my 3rd year of undergraduate studies with a CGPA of 3.446 and is expected to graduate in 2024.

I am willing to learn from companies and professionals in my field of work to improve my skillset as a developer.
