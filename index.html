About Me

I am Eryn Gabriel Tallador, a student from De La Salle University - Manila currently taking up Bachelor of Science in Interactive Entertainment - Major in Game Development. I am in my 4th year of undergraduate studies with a CGPA of 3.491 and am expected to graduate in 2024.

In our projects, I take pride on working with the frontend and backend. I mainly work on the User Interface and Audio Management in our projects although I also help out in coding the logic behind our games and layout ideas that would help the team in designing logic for futureproofing.

I am willing to learn from companies and professionals in my field of work to improve my skillset as a developer. I aim to explore the different sectors of game development as I consider these different types of knowledge to be beneficial in my own development as a better programmer and developer should I put myself in the perspective of different branches of development where my interests align such as designing.

Qualifications and Skills:

  • C, C++, and C# Programming
  • Unity

Portfolio

Here are my past projects that I have worked on throughout my current 3 year stay in De La Salle University

Pokemon Text-Based Game

  A Pokemon Text-Based Battle using C. First Machine Project created. Independent Project.

Minesweeper

  A minesweeper clone created for a machine project submission. Custom levels can be created and and saved through a text file. Uses C Language. Independent Project.

Games of the General

  Games of the General with AI Feature. Made for Artificial Intelligence class using Unity and C#. Mainly worked on the AI and majority in the UI and Audio. Made with Aldrey Gaurana

Lost in Gunkanjima

  A horror/suspense game in which the main premise is to find objects and give it to the headmaster. Mainly worked in the level design, lighting, terrain forming, and audio. Using Unity and C#. Made by Brooklyn Studios (Aldrey Gaurana, Emerson Celestial, Joseph Santos)

Home Alone Da Mountains

  A home-defense game where you repair and trap enemies. Main role is Audio Management and Fatigue Mechanic. Using Unity and C#. Made by Brooklyn Studios (Aldrey Gaurana, Emerson Celestial, Joseph Santos)

Alchemical Breakout

  An On-Rails Shooter Game similar to House of the Dead and Time Crisis. Main Role is Level Designing, Audio Management, Character On-Rails Movement using Navigation Mesh, and Enemy Spawning. Using Unity and C#. Made with Aldrey Gaurana.

Farrago

  A suspense puzzle game with the use of color mixing and problem solving. A work in progress and still in the middle of Alpha and Beta Version. Main role is Audio Management, Game System (Puzzle and Inventory), UI (Main Menu, Game Main UI, and Pause UI) and Graphics Settings System. Using Unity and C#. Made by [AniLu] Animus Ludus Studios (Aldrey Gaurana, Emerson Celestial, Joseph Santos, Ella Garcia, and Angelic Aram)

Unreal Challenge 1

  An ANNO1800 clone where we are tasked to do an efficient material production that can run without computation issues. Made in Unreal Engine and C++. Independent Project.

Pokemon Go Clone

  A Pokemon Go Clone to test features of Augmented Reality using Unity. Made in Unity, Vuforia for AR System, and C#. Main Role is the entire UI System and Encounter System. Made by Brooklyn Studios (Aldrey Gaurana, Emerson Celestial, Joseph Santos, Francis Santonia)
